The following statements are propositions, EXCEPT:
A. Euclid of Alexandria is human.\(\hspace{3cm}\:\)C. \(5 > 8\)
B. \(1 + 1 = 2\) \(\hspace{6.4cm}\)D. Who am I?

Definition 1.1 (Proposition) a statement that is either true or false (but not both).

Solution: “D. Who am I?” is not a proposition because interrogative statements have no truth value.

What is the question?

Solution: “I thoroughly disapprove of duels. If a man should challenge me, I would take him kindly and forgivingly by the hand and lead him to a quiet place and kill him.”
